Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Matrimony Platform – “Shaadi Connect”

1. Product Overview

Product Name

Shaadi Connect

Product Type

Web + Mobile Matrimony Platform

Vision

To help individuals and families discover meaningful life partners through a trusted, secure, and intelligent matchmaking platform.

Mission

Provide personalized matchmaking using preferences, compatibility scoring, and verified profiles while maintaining user privacy and trust.

---

2. Problem Statement

Traditional matchmaking methods are:

- Time-consuming
- Limited to social circles
- Lack compatibility insights
- Difficult for working professionals

Existing apps also face:

- Fake profiles
- Low trust
- Poor recommendation quality
- Privacy concerns

Users need:

- Verified profiles
- Better compatibility matching
- Family involvement options
- Safe communication channels

---

3. Goals & Objectives

- Increase user registrations

User Goals
